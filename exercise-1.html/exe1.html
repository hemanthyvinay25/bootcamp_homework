Chicken Pasta Soup

Warm Your Soul with this Comforting vegatable Parm Soup Recipe,
Guaranteed to Delight!
Spice Up Your Dinner Routine with Our Mouth-Watering Cajun Chicken Recipe!

Description

measurements ingredients
300gms dried penne
2 tsp olive oil
1 garlic clove crushed
75g baby spinach leaves
250g soft cheese
25g parmesan optional
450grms cooked chicken breast
100g frozen peas
1 bunch parsley or basil chopped

STEP 1 Cook the pasta following pack instructions. Reserve 100ml of the water and drain the pasta in a colander.

STEP 2 Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan on a medium heat and fry the garlic and spinach for 3 mins until wilted. Add the soft cheese and heat until melted. Stir in most of the parmesan, then add the shredded chicken, peas and some of the pasta water. Bring to the boil and bubble for 2-3 mins until the chicken and peas are completely heated through.

STEP 3 Add the pasta and stir until combined. Add more pasta water to loosen the sauce, if needed. Remove from heat, sprinkle over the remaining parmesan and parsley or basil to serve
